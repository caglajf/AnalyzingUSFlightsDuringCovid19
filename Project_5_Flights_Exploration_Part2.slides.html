Flights Dataset: Reporting Carrier On-Time Performance during Covid-19 Outbreak Exploration¶

by Çağla Odabaşı Özer¶

The U.S. Department of Transportation's (DOT) Bureau of Transportation Statistics (BTS) tracks the on-time performance of domestic flights operated by large air carriers. The dataset which reports flights in the United States, including carriers, arrival and departure delays, and reasons for delays between Jan, 2020 - March, 2020 (during Covid-19 outbreak) was downloaded and analyzed. The dataset was downloaded from the link below. https://www.transtats.bts.gov/DL_SelectFields.asp?Table_ID=
